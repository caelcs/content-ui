About Tidy Favs

Thinking of your needs.

What is Tidy Favs for?

Think about all the information you have stored over the years: notes, bookmarks, shopping lists, recipes, links, contacts, TO-DO lists and most of it spread among several applications. Tidy Favs is a way to store, tag and find your favorite content while giving you a great look and feel related to the content type.

Check your content in all your devices

All your content that you save will be notify to all your your mobile and tablet devices that are login.

What can you do with all your content?

With Tidy Favs you can share your content to your favorite social networks in a very nice format that you change and customise.
Export all your content in different format like PDF, CSV, DOC and many other formats.
